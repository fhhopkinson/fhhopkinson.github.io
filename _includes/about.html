About Me

A desire for a change of direction in my life led me to take General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course in order that I could work in web development.

I had previously been an Army Officer in both an operational and ceremonial context and subsequently I ran a gold mine before having a life changing accident which made me take a long hard look at my life and I realised that I was not making the most of it hence the pursuit of this new career.

Whilst the course has developed to prepare a graduate to be a full stack developer I feel that I could apply both my new skills from WDI and those that I have developed over my previous careers to become a Technical Project Manager.
